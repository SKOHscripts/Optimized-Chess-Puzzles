## üÜì Using Anki for Free on iPhone/iPad via Safari

You can use **AnkiWeb** through the **Safari** browser to review this deck for free on iOS.

### **Prerequisites**
1.  A free **[AnkiWeb](https://ankiweb.net/account/register)** account.
2.  The **Anki** app installed on your **computer** (Windows, macOS, Linux) for the initial deck import.

### **üì• Installation & Sync Procedure**

| Step | Action | Details |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **1.** | **Import the deck to your computer** | 1. Download the `.apkg` file from the [main project page](https://github.com/SKOHscripts/Optimized-Chess-Puzzles) or via [AnkiWeb](https://ankiweb.net/shared/info/894523279).<br>2. Open **Anki** on your computer.<br>3. Go to `File` ‚Üí `Import...` (or double-click the file).<br>4. Select the downloaded file. Your cards will be added to your local collection. |
| **2.** | **Sync with AnkiWeb** | 1. In Anki (on your computer), click the **sync button** (two circular arrows icon) in the top-right corner.<br>2. Log in with your AnkiWeb account.<br>3. Let the sync complete. The deck is now uploaded to AnkiWeb's servers. |
| **3.** | **Access cards on iPhone/iPad** | 1. Open **Safari** on your iPhone/iPad.<br>2. Go to [https://ankiweb.net/](https://ankiweb.net/) and log in.<br>3. Your collection, including the **"Optimized Chess Puzzles"** deck, is now accessible.<br>4. You can review directly in the browser. The interface is mobile-optimized. |

### **‚úÖ Advantages & Limitations of This Method**

| Advantage | Limitation |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Free.** | Requires an **active internet connection** to review (except for advanced browser offline mode usage). |
| No app installation needed on iOS. | User experience is **less fluid** and offers **fewer features** than the native AnkiMobile app. |
| Perfect sync with your computer collection. | Some advanced features (complex scripts) may not work exactly the same way. |

### **üîß Troubleshooting & Resources**

*   **Cards don't appear on AnkiWeb**: Verify the sync from your computer completed without errors.
*   **Chessboard display issues on mobile**: Refer to [issue #10](https://github.com/SKOHscripts/Optimized-Chess-Puzzles/issues/10) where fixes were discussed.
*   **Official AnkiWeb documentation**: [https://docs.ankiweb.net/](https://docs.ankiweb.net/).
